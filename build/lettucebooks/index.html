Lettuce books

Keep track of source documents and other material activities, so that tax time is a breeze.

Client responsibilities

  1. Upload source documents
  2. Answer questions supplied by bookkeeper
  3. Complete monthly questionnaire about material changes to business

Examples of questionnaire content.
  • Take out any new loans?
  • Make any large purchases over $500?
  • Did you land a large client?
  • Did you hire any sub-contractors?
  • Any abnormal spending this month?

Bookkeeper Responsibilities

Monthly

  1. Download source docs
  2. Review Entries
  3. Compile and post questions to Lettuce Books
  4. Review Client Questionnaire
  5. Adjust QuickBooks accordingly (based off client questions & questionaire)
  6. Upload Financials
  7. Upload Invoice to Customer (pdf upload from QB)

Annual

  1. Provide tax document assistance.
  2. Provide annual summary for CPA
