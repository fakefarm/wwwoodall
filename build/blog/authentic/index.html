Boulder doesn't have palm trees.

Feb 10, 2015

People work with people they like and trust. So how can branding help build like and trust?

With small business branding, how close is the name to you, the founder. The human subconscious has the ability to feel out a situation and know if it's sketchy or not. Mike is in Coronado, Ca and the theme is allowed to be nautical, navy, and ocean. But me in Boulder, I can't have palm trees in my logo or people are going to wonder because it's out of place.

It matters because a new brand needs to look authentic so people can feel you are authentic. I use the term Authentic as in there are no surprises and thus can give more of my trust. If you shoot straight with your branding then I think you'll shoot straight with me.
