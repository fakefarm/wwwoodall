HUG TIME!

Mar 04, 2015

I set an alarm for 7:23 pm to remind me to hug my family. Yes, I'm that pathetic. I've been pleasantly surprised with how much it changes my perspective from the chore at hand to realizing these children and Danae will be grown and gone before I know it. Now that Oscar knows what the alarm means he shouts out "HUG TIME!" when he hears it which means it's here to stay.
