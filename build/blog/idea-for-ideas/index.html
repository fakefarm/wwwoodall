An idea for ideas

Feb 05, 2015

I am regularly looking for building projects to develop my coding skills. I've shipped a few apps that I'm proud of including Destroy All Tasks and Afterburners. I confess they are both in near abandon-ware but they had actual users at one time!

I regularly have new ideas. So much so that I've started to lose track of them. So I've decided to let my newest learning project be a site about ideas. I'm thinking of it as 'Open Source Business Development'. Where, users will provide their idea and other users can contribute with MORE than just comments and likes. The crowd will upload art, business, and code to support the idea. Idea people get some help and aspiring designers, developers, and MBA's practice their skills. I could see this as a crowd-sourced sweat-equity platform.

So, this is my third app I'm going to build with end-users in mind and I've decided to blog my progress.

PS - If you subscribe I'll send you updates as they come.
