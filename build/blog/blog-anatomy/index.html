Blog Anatomy

Dec 10, 2014

I'm building my own blog so it gave me reason to study what the big blogs offer. I looked at about 30 different blogs and listed what they have in common.

Common traits

marketing

  • social links
  • email signup
  • twitter feeds

revenue

  • ads

Content Navigation

  • popular this week links
  • greatest hits
  • archive by date
  • search bar

Site nav

  • about
  • contact

sub navigation

  • links to other blogs
  • index of all blog
  • tag cloud

engagement

  • comments
  • call to action - submit a story or suggestion!

content

  • assets like the fart chart
  • featured content
  • amazon product ads
  • video of the day
  • pop up ads
  • list of contributors and their contact info
  • list of awards
  • list of recent comments


Topics

Blog content is all across the board and covers most of these things;

  • news
  • opinion
  • reviews
  • projects
  • ideas
  • store
  • sports
  • creativity
  • live survey


Content DNA

What I found a blog post needs to be is the following;

  • shareable
  • approachable
  • commentable
  • pretty
  • easy


Getting Traffic

I'm not entirely sure how to stand out but I think that it's a bit a of chicken and egg. In one way you need a blog to get traffic. From what I can see here are a few ways to get eyeballs

  • contribute to other blogs
  • social
  • pay for ads
  • contributors on our site
  • email list


What is our service?

I also noticed that blogs have different purposes. I find it will help to know why we blog. Here are the various reasons I see people blogging;

  • entertainment
  • ideas
  • inspiration
  • education
  • assets
  • resources
  • opinion of reviews
  • context
