Top podcasts

Mar 14, 2015

It's 2015 and I am only now getting into podcasts. So, I asked 6 friends their favorites and this is the list. I'm waiting on a few and I'm also going to have each on link to it's show when this post is finalized…

List (Still in draft)

  • Advanced Selling Podcast (yes, it's for sales peeps).

  • Entrepreneurial Thought Leadership - Stanford University

  • a16z - podcast of Venture Capital firm, Andressen Horowitz

  • Dave Ramsey - can't help myself, but this is just entertaining salt of earth reminders on the value of frugality (even if he does view al debt as the devil).

  • 60 Minutes. Love it.

  • The Deeply Graphic Design Cast— 3 designers who talk about their approach and experiences as running their own design shops.

  • Design Matters with Debbie Millman— Branding and design talk

  • Seanwes Podcast— A self-made successful designer. business related conversations about pursuing your art and building a business around it.

  • Entre Family— Being entrepreneurs as a family

  • This Is Your Life, with Michael Hyatt— business related talk

  • The Portfolio Life, with Jeff Goins— business/ pursuing your craft/passion

  • The Suitcase Entrepreneur— Doing your business while traveling.

  • Lets Make Mistakes— Just added it. I have read and like his books, Successful Design firm talk about business and being a designer.

  • Men In Blazers — Yes, my absolute favorite podcast is two bald British men discussing soccer and Downton Abbey.

  • Howler Radio & Dummy — Another great soccer podcast.

  • Foundation — Kevin Rose interviewing founders.

  • The Tim Ferriss Show — Interviews with successful individual, deconstructing their success.

  • The Random Show — Tim Ferriss & Kevin Rose discussing random shit.

  • Monocle Films — Short films by my favorite magazine. I also highly recommend their radio station, Monocle 24.

  • Style Guide Podcast — Brad Frost discussing style guides with designers.

  • Design Details — I'm still warming up to this one. Product designers discussing their craft.

  • The 99u Podcast — Interviews with creatives, thinkers and entrepreneurs.

  • On Being: This is a podcast of Krista Tippett from NPR who interviews people of all different faith backgrounds. It is not only inspirational, it is grounding and eye opening. Krista is probably one of the best interviewers out there and she has an incredible list of people she has interviewed.

  • Serial: Need I say more? It is addictive, gets you thinking and is AWESOME.

  • 10xTalk: This is a podcast for highly successful entrepreneurs looking to for exponential growth. It is hosted by Dan Sullivan, Founder of Strategic Coach and Joe - Pollish, an Marketing guru.

  • Stuff you Should Know. A brilliant podcast of random facts about things we should (read find it interesting) to know.

  • Strategic Entrepreneur Podcast (This is a shameless plug as it is my podcast). It is a podcast on intentional learning, leading and life. My co-host Tim and I share from our experience as serial entrepreneurs to start ups and early stage businesses. :)

  • The Gist

  • Daring Fireball

  • Here's the thing

  • This American Life

  • Serial

  • Freakonomics

  • Planet Money

  • Back to Work
