How I blog

Dec 24, 2014

There are no rules to blogging. The point of a blog is you say whatever you want. I used to think of blogging as a formal process. I suppose I got sucked into this whole professional formula that needed to have all these various things lined up; Audience, consistency, and buzz words for the search engines. I gave up on that.

This blog is a collection of thoughts in the moment. I've built this blog in such a way that when I have an idea or learn a lesson then I make a quick post about it.

So unless otherwise stated, every post is a work in progress.
