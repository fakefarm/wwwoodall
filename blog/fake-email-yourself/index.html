fake email yourself

Feb 17, 2015

Here's my latest way to blogâ€¦

I take a lot of notes. It's one of the perks of working with so many great people. Often times my co-workers mention posts or subjects that I want to research. These notes are not private information which end up sitting on my desk and clutting it up.

Lately I've been thinking about improving my recording system. You know how a normal post-meeting action item is to send a summary email of what happened and TODOs. Well, I'm taking that view and writing an email to myself. It just happens to be posted to my blog.

Why I do this is because it's fresh on my mind. As much as I think I'll enter it later, it won't happen. Recording things in short, fake email like posts keeps them better organized because of tagging. I also like to think it shows other people the kind of things I'm learning. I also like that I can link it to whomever to comment and let me know if what I'm thinking is wrong :-)
