Selling the Catholic Church

Nov 28, 2014

Top Level Domains (TLD's) are the end of domain names like; .com and.net. But new this are hundreds of new TLD's were released. Domains like;

  • .surf
  • .church
  • .bike
  • .accounting

Meaning, instead of www.boulderBikes.com, you could use www.boulder.bike. The idea is that the ending of the domain can help describe the kind of business.

I totally got sucked into a land grab mentality and ended up buying about 10 or so TLD's including. Some I'm trying to sell;

These are For Sale

As I sit on these amazing domains I wonder how does one go about selling domains?

Other than listing them in aution sites, I came up with another idea. What if I create a site called the 'The TLD official color guide'. It will just be a list of TLD's like .church, .life, .biz and some accompanying color. Why is it the official color? Because I say so and nobody else is making a site that says otherwise :-)

Then, at the bottom I can have my list of domains for sell.

Think it could work?
