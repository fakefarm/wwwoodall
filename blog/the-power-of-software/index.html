The Power of Software

Jan 08, 2015

I've been a developer at LivingSocial for a year now. It's my first corporate, full time software job. During that time, my primary focus was getting acclimated to the code base, writing code, and working to get better as a developer. In short, I had my head buried in the WHAT of software.

The team I'm on is not typical CRUD. It's more advanced subject matter and I didn't see the big picture. Only recently have I begun to apply the software We've been building. The application of my work was eye opening to how profoundly powerful software is to a company and those who know how to write, and employ it.
