Starting a business

Jan 05, 2015

If you think you need money, business partner, then you haven't found your business yet. If you do something because it's fun and you could make money at it then you have some ideas.

Jim Collins Describes the Hedgehog concept in his book good to great. They call it the hedgehog concept that you find something you love passionately, you're good at, and you can make money doing. I didn't take this seriously until I founded Feather. And unfortunately, it was hard to see that these things are real concepts. If you don't like talking, then you won't like sales. If you don't like blogging, then you sholdn't become a writer.

I created a theory about business that I went out and tested. I had my own three circles - market, scientist, natural resource. The problem is the scientist ended up getting the business. Or, I gave it to him because I would have rather that than continue. The truth is you need to be your own scientist.
