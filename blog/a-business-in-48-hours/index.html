a business in 48 hours

Dec 26, 2014

Merry Christmas!

We celebrated in normal fashion - up wrapping presents until 2am on Christmas Eve for the kids. Pretty much anything that can qualify to be wrapped; Crayons.

  • Danae asked for a coffee maker
  • Christmas is about opening presents
  • Cash is no where near the spirit of opening a gift
  • I wanted both the experience of gifts and the flexibility of cash.

  • I also didn't want to buy something for spending sake

  • so I made these cards
  • she loved them

  • I got thinking that maybe other people could benefit and agree that gift cards are sterile way to show appreciation.

  • I came up with 'feather' as a name because they are unique and symbolize faith, hope, and love.
  • I bought feather.cards
  • I created a gumroad page
  • I posted photos on middleman
  • in 48 hours I have a bad site up

This project is unique in that I can design the cards, build the site, and print out orders in an Just in time process at the local fedex office.

Setup via one.

I bought a printer but did a rookie move of getting an ink jet. I also tried some card stock on it and that paper was bad. So, until further notice, I'm going to print at fedex and get a scanner or just take photos with my phone.
