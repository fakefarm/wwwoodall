3 minutes

Jan 02, 2015

I was sitting in the car with my brother-in-law and the time was 7:21pm. I wanted to explain to him the difference between three minutes.

The future is one minute from now, at 7:22. You have the ability to influence what happens during 7:22, but you cannot change what happened in 7:20. All you can do with 7:20 is take the lessons you learned and let them influence how you live 7:22. Use now, 7:21, to make 7:22 what you want it to be.
